Otter is a 4 year old Golden Retriever mix. He is 25% Golden Retriever, Boxer, Rottweiler, and Australian Cattle Dog. He has a condition called progressive retinal atrophy (PRA) that results in blindness. He is the sweetest dog, loves his stuffed animals and going on walks. He doesn't let his blindness hold him back!
